Moongle Farm is an online virtual world platform combined with Metaverse and NFT (Non-Fungible Token) technology. The platform aims to connect the field of agriculture in the Metaverse with the real world. The platform aims to connect the field of agriculture in the Metaverse with the real world. Moongle Farm's mission is to enable users to build their second lives on the Metaverse with agricultural-related activities.

Bringing people closer to the Metaverse

At Moongle Farm, users can construct and manage their own farms, participate in agricultural activities, and bridge the gap between real life and the Metaverse. The main technologies used include Solidity, React Native, AI, and AR/VR.

Bringing people closer to the Metaverse, Moongle Farm offers cutting-edge features that include:

  • NFT Marketplace
  • AI Virtual Assistant
  • Augmented Reality/Virtual Reality (AR/VR) Technology: MoongleFarm integrates AR/VR technology, enabling users to experience immersive virtual environments within the Metaverse. This technology enhances the overall user experience and provides a more interactive and engaging platform.
